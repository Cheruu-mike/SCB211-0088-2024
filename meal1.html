Chicken

Ingredients:

  • Garlic
  • Paprika
  • Lemon juice

Preparation Steps:

Step-by-step instructions on how to prepare the meal.

Marinate – Mix garlic (minced), paprika, lemon juice, salt, and a bit of oil. Coat the chicken and let it sit for at least 30 minutes. Cook – Grill, pan-fry, or bake at 180°C (350°F) for about 25-30 minutes, turning occasionally. Serve – Let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing. Serve with your preferred sides.
